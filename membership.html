our membership

Games for Change (G4C) is dedicated to harnessing the transformative power of games and immersive media to address pressing social challenges and catalyze positive change in communities worldwide. Our mission is to empower game creators, educators, policymakers, and innovators to leverage the unique capabilities of games for education, healthcare, and social justice.
At the core of our work is the belief that games are more than entertainmentâ€”they are powerful tools for fostering empathy, promoting learning, and driving social impact. Through our initiatives, programs, and collaborations, we strive to build a global community of changemakers who are passionate about using games as a force for good.
With a focus on innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity, G4C continues to push the boundaries of what games can achieve and inspire a new generation of creators and players to join us in the journey towards a more equitable and compassionate world.
Our ultimate goal is to create a world where games are recognized as a legitimate and impactful medium for addressing complex social issues, driving meaningful engagement, and inspiring positive action. We envision a future where game developers, educators, and policymakers work hand-in-hand to harness the full potential of games to tackle some of the most pressing challenges facing our society. >
