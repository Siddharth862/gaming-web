about us

Games for Change (G4C) is the world’s largest nonprofit membership organization dedicated to serving all individuals who leverage games for social impact and positive change.

We bring together developers at key industry conferences and in over 150 chapters and special interest groups (SIGs) to improve their lives and their craft. Whether you’re a student with a goal to become a game developer or a seasoned veteran; whether you’re based in the United States or any other country or region in the world.
